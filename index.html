Era Tactics

Turn-based strategy game for Android

About

Take control of a team of adventurers from all periods of history: These “eras” include the past, the present, and the future!
Battle through levels of enemies, accumulating experience and new equipment to improve your heroes.
Battles take place by pitting your 3x3 formation of heroes against various 3x3 groups of enemies. Finding victory depends on good positioning of your forces, advancing into the enemy forces to defeat them.

This game was created for Mike Scott's Mobile computing course.
